Fragments of a Stoic Lifestyle

Stoic Logic.

Grounding every action—from debugging complex C++ architectures to interpreting life's chaos—in the principles of Stoicism. Focus only on what can be controlled.

Dark Minimal.

A preference for oversized silhouettes, earth tones, and unbranded functional pieces. Maintaining a sharp, consistent visual language in everyday wear.

Iron & Ritual.

Physical health dictates mental acuity. A rigid structure of cutting phases, protein tracking, and relentless consistency in the weight room.

Details & Motion.

Whether navigating the pulse of the city after hours, or appreciating the intricate mechanics of a custom timepiece—true sophistication always lies in the unseen details.
